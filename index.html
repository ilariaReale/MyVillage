The Problem

Modern life broke the village.

Three separate crises — unaffordable childcare, senior isolation, and fragmented neighborhoods — are actually one problem: we lost the village. And no app has tried to rebuild it.

💸

Childcare is unaffordable

$30–$50/hr for a babysitter. Over $3,500/month for two kids in daycare in Seattle. Families are stretched to the breaking point — and many are dropping out of the workforce.

🧓

Seniors are isolated

Friends pass away. Children move cities. Seniors have time, wisdom, and love to give — but no infrastructure to share it. Loneliness is now a public health crisis.

🌍

Immigrants raise kids alone

Grandparents are on another continent. No aunts, no cousins, no family safety net. Communities that should form naturally — don't.

🏘️

Neighbors are strangers

We live next door to each other for years without knowing each other's names. Real community doesn't happen by accident anymore — it needs a platform.

Who It's For

Four people. One village.

My Village brings together people who have complementary needs and turns them into a real community.

👨‍👩‍👧

Families

Parents who need childcare support, playdates, and community — without paying a fortune for strangers.

🧓

Seniors

Retirees with time, warmth, and skills to share — looking for purpose and genuine human connection.

😊

Individuals

People new to a city, navigating a life transition, or simply wanting to put down roots in their neighborhood.

🤝

Caregivers

People who offer help — cooking, language exchange, babysitting, errands — and want to be part of a trusted network.

The Solution

My Village: a community platform built on who you are, not what you do.

Instead of a work resume, your profile reflects your passions, origins, availability, and what you can offer. The app finds genuine matches nearby and helps you build real relationships — not transactions.

Discover Neighbors

Browse nearby families, seniors, and individuals by type. Get a "neighbor who'd love to meet you" suggestion every day.

Village Events

Create and join local events — Swaps, Strolls, Playdates, Meals, Garden days. Meet in real life, not just online.

Direct Messaging

Chat directly with your neighbors. Coordinate a cooking session, a pickup, a shared afternoon — naturally.

Village Score

Track your connections, offers made, and Village Score. A reputation system built on generosity, not followers.

How It Works

Simple by design. Meaningful by nature.

Three steps to find your village.

1

Create your profile

Share who you are — your origins, languages, passions, what you can offer, and what you're looking for.

2

Get matched locally

We connect you with compatible people nearby — a senior who loves kids, a neighbor who speaks your language, a family that needs what you offer.

3

Build your village

A first meeting, a shared meal, an afternoon with the kids. Real relationships — not transactions. Your village grows over time.

Our Vision

More than an app. A new kind of community infrastructure.

Think of it as the digital version of how communities used to work — word of mouth, trust, reciprocity. Where you could knock on a neighbor's door and ask for help. Where an elder was a resource, not a burden.

We're building the platform that makes that possible again — at scale, across cities, for everyone who's ever felt that raising kids or growing old shouldn't be this lonely.
